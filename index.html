Daechan Kim is a multidisciplinary designer who creates products with intentional interactions that enrich everyday experiences.

Originally from South Korea and formally trained in the United States as an industrial designer, he expresses a unique balance of subtlety and clarity through honest materiality and communicative form.

Grounded in a close observation of human behavior, his practice extends across product, service, and experience design to distill complexity into clear, intuitive forms.

Approaching design as a foundational language, he creates objects that genuinely invite interaction, bringing function, human perception, and emotion into quiet alignment in object.
